The Annual Meeting is the PTO's main yearly business meeting. Members review the proposed budget, hold board elections, hear annual reports, ask questions, and vote on business requiring membership approval.

    <div><span>March</span><p>Budget review and election discussion begin.</p></div>
    <div><span>Spring</span><p>Members receive information, ask questions, and prepare for the Annual Meeting.</p></div>
