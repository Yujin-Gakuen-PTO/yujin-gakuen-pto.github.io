Jikyuso Triathlon, YG's jog-a-thon, combines movement and reading. Students may collect pledges for each lap, each English page read, each Japanese page read, or as a flat donation.

Fundraising is not required for students to participate.

## Where the funds go

Jikyuso funds support Yujin Gakuen Associated Student Body accounts rather than the PTO. School staff lead the event, and the PTO helps families find volunteer and participation information when it is available.

## Volunteering

Volunteers are needed during school hours. The signup and grade schedule will appear after the school confirms them.
