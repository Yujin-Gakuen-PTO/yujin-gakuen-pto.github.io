The Caring Crane Fund provides confidential, need-based support to students and families in the Yujin Gakuen community.

Donations stay within the school community and are maintained separately from the PTO operating budget. Support may include grocery or gas cards, essential items, or other assistance determined by YG administration.

## How support is handled

YG administration identifies needs, determines eligibility, approves requests, and directs assistance. PTO members do not review requests, vote on assistance, or receive the identities of families who receive help.

## Ask for help

Families may speak with Kocho-Sensei Kimberly Ingram, Brigitte Phipps, Scott Klein, or their student's teacher. Assistance is handled confidentially.
