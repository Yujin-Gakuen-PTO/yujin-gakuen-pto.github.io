The UO Beverage Booth is the PTO's largest fundraiser. Revenue supports Japanese interns, field trips, classroom supplies, cultural events, and other programs for YG students and staff. If the PTO cannot staff the booth, it risks losing this fundraising opportunity.

## How to volunteer

<ol class="process-steps">
  <li>
    <span>1</span>
    <div><h3>Check that the shift fits</h3><p>Volunteers must be at least 21 years old and hold a valid OLCC Alcohol Service Permit. Volunteers may be parents, guardians, relatives, neighbors, family friends, or other trusted adults.</p></div>
  </li>
  <li>
    <span>2</span>
    <div><h3>Take an alcohol-server course</h3><p>The PTO recommends an online course that takes about 1.5 hours. Other approved course providers may also be available.</p><p><a href="{{ site.data.links.olcc_course.url }}">{{ site.data.links.olcc_course.label }}</a></p></div>
  </li>
  <li>
    <span>3</span>
    <div><h3>Apply through OLCC CAMP</h3><p>Create or open your CAMP account, select <strong>Permitting and Education</strong>, choose <strong>Apply for Permit</strong>, then select <strong>Alcohol Service Permit</strong>.</p><p><a href="{{ site.data.links.olcc_camp.url }}">{{ site.data.links.olcc_camp.label }}</a></p></div>
  </li>
  <li>
    <span>4</span>
    <div><h3>Request reimbursement</h3><p>The PTO reimburses the permit cost. Email the booth coordinator after completing the process for the current reimbursement instructions.</p><p><a href="mailto:booth@yujingakuenpto.com?subject=OLCC%20permit%20reimbursement">Request OLCC reimbursement</a></p></div>
  </li>
  <li>
    <span>5</span>
    <div><h3>Choose your games</h3><p>Each home game has its own signup. Select as many dates as you can, then watch for shift details from the booth coordinator.</p></div>
  </li>
</ol>
