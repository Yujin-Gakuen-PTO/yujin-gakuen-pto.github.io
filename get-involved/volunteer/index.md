PTO events depend on volunteers from across the YG community. Families, staff, and community partners help run activities, staff fundraisers, prepare supplies, set up, and clean up. Without enough event-day help, some activities cannot happen safely or at all.

<div class="volunteer-overview">
  <section>
    <span class="badge">Fundraising shifts</span>
    <h2>Fund programs across YG</h2>
    <p>The UO Beverage Booth is the PTO's largest fundraiser. It accounts for about 80% of annual fundraising and supports interns, field trips, classroom supplies, cultural events, and other help for YG students and staff.</p>
    <a href="{{ '/get-involved/uo-beverage-booth/' | relative_url }}">How the UO Beverage Booth works</a>
  </section>
  <section>
    <span class="badge">Event-day help</span>
    <h2>Help an event happen</h2>
    <p>These are defined shifts such as running an activity, giving out prizes, preparing a space, greeting families, or cleaning up. You do not need to join a planning committee first.</p>
  </section>
  <section>
    <span class="badge">Committee planning</span>
    <h2>Want to shape the event?</h2>
    <p>Committees meet before an event to make plans, coordinate roles, and make decisions. That is a different commitment from taking one volunteer shift.</p>
    <a href="{{ '/get-involved/committees/' | relative_url }}">See committee opportunities</a>
  </section>
