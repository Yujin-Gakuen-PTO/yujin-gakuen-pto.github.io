    <h2>Why do I need a separate PTO form?</h2>
    <p>Because Yujin Gakuen and Yujin Gakuen PTO are separate organizations. School enrollment does not create PTO membership or give the PTO permission to add an adult to its communication list.</p>
  </div>
  <p class="join-explainer__aside"><strong>You are not joining a mysterious compulsory parent society.</strong> You are giving a separate nonprofit permission to contact you and creating your own membership record.</p>
</div>

## What joining does

Each parent, guardian, or staff member completes the form individually. The PTO uses the information for communication and volunteer coordination, and each completed adult membership carries its own voting rights.

PTO emails include meeting information, agendas, event updates, volunteer opportunities, and other timely family information.

## What joining does not do

Joining does not obligate you to attend meetings, volunteer, serve on a committee, donate, or take on a leadership role. Those choices remain optional.
