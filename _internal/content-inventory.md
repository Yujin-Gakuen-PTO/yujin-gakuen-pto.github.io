## Publication Boundaries

- Draft minutes do not publish.
- Combined files that expose draft minutes do not publish as agenda links.
- Budget proposals, figures, and approval claims wait for public approval.
- Tax identifiers, private contacts, and unfinished internal governance drafts do not publish.
- Command Center findings remain advisory until Hannah confirms them or a current public-ready source supports them.
